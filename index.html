// FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to know how to code? +

No. I handle all the technical setup. You just tell me what you want automated and I build it. I also include training so you can make small changes yourself if you want.

What if I don't have a powerful computer? +

No problem. I use Ollama Pro Cloud ($20/month) which handles the heavy AI processing in the cloud. Your local machine just runs the automation scripts and interface. Any modern laptop works.

Is my data really private? +

Yes. Everything runs on your machine. Your data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly choose cloud features. No third-party AI companies see your information. You're in full control.

What happens after the 30-day support? +

You can email me anytime with questions. For ongoing changes and new features, I offer monthly plans ($89-$149/mo) that include weekly optimization and priority support. Or you can reach out for one-off updates at my hourly rate.

How long does setup take? +

Most setups are done in 1-3 days. Complex systems with multiple integrations might take up to a week. I'll give you a clear timeline during our discovery call.
